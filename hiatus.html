Hiatus

I have been putting off writing this first blog post for the better part of two years. A lack of focus is probably the main reason why. Its 2021, we all have ADHD and I am no exception to the rule. How strange that a diagnosis could be so pervasive to modern society. Blame the lizard shape shifting Zuckerberg I say.

Either way, let’s stay on task. What is this blog for? I named it as the Ignorant View, for the reason that I am ignorant in everything I do. ‘If there is one thing I know, is that I know nothing at all’. – Socrates. Yes…yes, I will shamelessly insert a cliché quote where possible. Get used to it.

And I do hope you do get used it. I plan to pump these ‘things’ twice every week. A pact to myself. These ‘things’ will mostly cover my experiences and journey to financial freedom. A sort of documentation. I have absolute no idea where I want to take this, but I hope we can find out over time.

Right, so a bit about myself. Lets get this out in one go because I really avoid talking about by myself (says the person starting a blog). Take a deep breath, and….

I am a 22 year old, Black British History graduate who is currently in their second year of teaching Design and Technology in a secondary school (it makes sense, read it again), who is quite introverted but likes a bit of socialising until my social battery depletes, so like most people, I am a walking contradiction who seeks to be outraged, and other than that I am really just a normal, average person who is still wondering why they didn’t buy bitcoin in 2013 (we all had the chance, even my pubescent self).

Now that the vanity is out the way, as mentioned, this thing will generally focus on my attempt to achieve financial freedom. We have heard the term before and I am sure I am not the only one who has tried to sell you a Forex trading course or help set up your own shopify/dropshipping business from home. No, this is a narcissistic narration of my journey from one shiny object to another, stopping by financial Tik Tok teenfluencers along the way. To premise the pilot episode of this thing, I will catch you up on the last two years I was on hiatus (lets hope this isn’t an anime and doesn’t happen again).
